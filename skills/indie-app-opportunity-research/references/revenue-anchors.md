### Pattern
- One standalone AI app typically needs 1–3 months to show revenue.
- Portfolios of 5–8 apps hit an inflection point via cross-promotion.
- Ticket size is usually $4.99–$9.99/mo or a $6.99 lifetime unlock.

## How to use these
- For MRR estimation: find the closest comparable in the table by app category + monetization model, then halve/double depending on ASO difficulty signal.
- For investor/partner conversations: cite **Verified Public Revenue** only. AI Wrapper anchors are softer (founder-self-reported, single-tweet citations).
- For BUILD/DROP decisions: a niche with no comparable anchor in either table = treat with extra caution. Build only if community signal (Phase 3) is unusually strong.
